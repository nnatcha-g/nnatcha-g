## About Me

I'm a fourth-year ICT student at Mahidol University, majoring in Multimedia Systems, with a focus on game development, interactive media, serious games, and applied AI.

My work usually lives somewhere between:

- psychological horror and symbolic storytelling  
- Unity/C# gameplay systems  
- educational and healthcare-oriented interactive applications  
- player psychology, fear design, and research-informed game design  
- student leadership, teaching, and community-building through game development  

I’m especially interested in using games and immersive media beyond entertainment, including education, healthcare, training, and meaningful interactive experiences.

## Featured Projects

### Sennethia — 2D Psychological Horror Game  
**Game Director & Lead Programmer**  
A Unity-based psychological horror game focused on symbolic storytelling, atmospheric horror, ritual-based mechanics, and puzzle-driven exploration.

### Lumie — Mobile Application for Collecting Blink Videos  
**Project Manager & Unity Developer**  
A Unity Android application designed to support guided blink video recording in ophthalmology outpatient workflows using camera recording, face/eye landmark guidance, quality checking, metadata logging, and video handoff.

### Project Lunar — Voxel VR Horror Experience  
**Game Director & Lead Programmer**  
A short voxel-based VR horror experience built with Godot, OpenXR, and MagicaVoxel, exploring presence-based fear through spatial layout, lighting, analog-horror interface design, and environmental storytelling.

### Guardians of the Wildz — Unity Collectible Card Game  
**Project Manager & Unity Developer**  
A Unity-based digital collectible card game focused on environmental awareness and trash management. Received Honorable Mention at the 26th National Software Contest 2024.

### Bayesian Network Model for Player Fear Level Prediction  
**Developer**  
An AI-based model that predicts player fear levels based on psychological horror game design elements, connecting game design, player psychology, and data-informed design.

### The Night Before Twenty-One — Interactive Memoir  
**Game Designer & Developer**  
A web-based interactive memoir transforming poetry and personal reflection into an interactive art experience using HTML, CSS, and web-based storytelling.
